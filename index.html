Tons of Art Classes Available!

Got art block or just want to try something fun and new? Come on in and get comfy at the Creative Corner where everyone's an artist! The Creative Corner offers a wide variety of classes ranging from painting to pottery and everything else in between! Don't currently see a class that you're interested in? Contact us and let us know what other classes you'd like us to offer!

Whether you've never picked up a paintbrush, or have a fully stocked art studio of your own, our classes offer an incredible experience for artists of every background and every age! The more you are able to appreciate and understand the art of different eras, movements, styles and techniques, the better you can develop, evaluate and improve your own artwork.

Founded in 2002, the Creative Corner seeks to develop Bryan and College Station-area artists through its studio art classes and workshops. Each year, over 1,200 adult students of diverse backgrounds, skill levels, and ages enroll in over 100 classes and workshops annually at the Creative Corner. Led by professional artist instructors, classes and workshops take place one fully equipped studio space where students work in painting, drawing, origami, printmaking, woodworking, and pottery. To ensure individual attention and to accommodate varying skill levels, courses are maintained with a limited number of students. There are no prerequisites for enrollment and courses are offered at an affordable cost to allow broad access to anyone interested. Students also have access to any paid exhibitions, lectures, public programs, and artist talks offered throughout the year to further their training as artists.

Events & Gallery Viewings

The Creative Corner is also a gallery and event space. Explore our rotating exhibits of fine art from around the world or create a masterpiece of your own with our tailor-made art classes! All artists are welcome in applying to have their work showcased on our walls. We would love to host your next event! See from the list of current events happening soon and feel free to contact us to arrange a private event.

The Creative Corner offers monthly juried exhibits for exhibiting artist members to enter. All exhibits are free and open to the public. Rare private exhibits may be visited for a fee, unless already a student of the Creative Corner.
